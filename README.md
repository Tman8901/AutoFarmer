# Auto Farmer

## Farming in Minecraft was just made easier.

This mod implements parts of WAILA (What am I looking at) and Baritone to try and make the perfect farming experience in survival!

Big shout out to RiceFarmer11 (github.com/familiar) for inspiring this idea with his sunsi bot!

## Features
- Auto combat to prevent deaths
- Auto-eat to prevent hunger
- Auto farm and replant
- Auto sell to villagers  (EXTRA SETUP REQUIRED)*

## How to set up
Download Fabric from fabricmc.net and follow the download instructions from there.
Once you've done that, download the .jar file from the latest releases or (Modrinth link) and drag the file into your mods' folder.
Don't know how to get to your mods' folder?

On your keyboard, press the Windows key + R and type %AppData%, click your .minecraft folder, then click on mods and drag the file into there.

    <img width="343" height="324" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6a7a4f82-bd5a-4455-a752-945b7a2aed17" />

Make sure that your inventory is set up like this with your food in your offhand and your hoe/farming equipment of choice in the first slot.

## Steps to farm
1. Go to the start of your farm and do /setPosition
2. Walk your farm like you would usually farm it
3. When you reach the end of your farm, do /setPosition again
4. Clear your inventory except for the required itmes and start farming by pressing F8!

The bot will try its best to run its quickest and most efficient. Tracking blocks/s and emeralds/h.

## *How to set up auto sell
Place 1-2 villagers every 100 blocks of the farm and do /setVillagerPosition. The bot will save its location in /minecraft/config/autofarm.
**MAKE SURE IT HAS THE RIGHT TRADES FOR THE SPECIFIC CROP**
    
### The default keybind to access the mod is O, but you can always type /autofarmer to acess the mod at any time.

Happy Farming!
